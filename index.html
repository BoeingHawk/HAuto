About-Us

Devoted to offer highest quality products incorporated in the year 1970 at Paonta Sahib (Himachal Pradesh, India) and got registered on 15 October 1974 with DIC as reg. no. (06)(10)(01342) and since then we are serving in this industry with consistent performance. We work for speacialized fabrication for Stainless Steel and Pharma Machinery Parts. Growing as a well established manufacturer, we have adopted all the quality management techniques to deliver high quality products. We also have put forward our own range of machines and apllied products, that assures the products of finest quality giving clients maximum output for their requirements.
